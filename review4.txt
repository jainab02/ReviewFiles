Languages Cuisine of india:
India is a linguistically diverse country, with over 1,600 languages spoken across its regions. Hindi and English are the official languages at the national level, but each state has its own official language(s). Some of the most widely spoken languages include Hindi, Bengali, Telugu, Marathi, Tamil, Urdu, Gujarati, Kannada, and Punjabi.Indian cuisine is celebrated worldwide for its flavors, spices, and regional specialties. From aromatic curries to savory snacks and sweet desserts, Indian food offers a tantalizing array of tastes and textures. Each region has its own culinary traditions and signature dishes. Some popular Indian dishes include biryani, butter chicken, dosa, samosa, paneer tikka, and various types of flavorful curries.
